Kittanny´s River Campground

Milford, PA

Kittanny´s River Campground is the premier campground on the Milford River in the Mountains. This location consists of over 160 sites built on 18 acres of beautiful wooded grounds. Catering to both tent and recreational vehicles, this facility provides our guests with river trips, waterfront tent camping, 2 full service bathhouses, laundromat and a beautiful camp store. WIFI is available while relaxing on the porch of our camp store. Camp store carries a variety of basic camping supplies, clothing and river trip gear as well as used canoes and kayaks. Must be 21 years of age or older, or be accompanied by an adult to camp at either of our campgrounds. Holiday periods require 3 night minimum stay, and an activity for at least 80% of group. Check out our website for more details and a campground map!
