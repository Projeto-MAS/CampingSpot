Gerês River Park

Braga, Portugal

Created by mountaineers for mountaineers, Gerês River Park is your "base camp" for all mountain activities in Serra do Gerês. Located in one of the last community villages in our country, it also aims to make known ancestral uses and customs, such as the transhumance of cattle, "vezeira", the "wheel" of goats, the "aviar" of water, as well as promoting tasty " night at the community mill", where everyone can participate in the olive oil process and taste it first hand in a typical village dish.
